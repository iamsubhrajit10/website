Bio

August 15, 2024 · Last updated on: February 18, 2025 · 1 min · 110 words · Subhrajit

Subhrajit Das is a final-year M.Tech student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Gandhinagar. His current research focuses on Performance Optimization and Usable Privacy & Security. Before joining IIT Gandhinagar, he completed an M.Sc in Computer Science from the University of Kalyani and a B.Sc (Honours) in Computer Science from West Bengal State University. Alongside his studies, he has been a Teaching Assistant at IIT Gandhinagar for courses such as Distributed Systems, Computer & Network Security, Compilers, and Data Structures & Algorithms. He also co-instructed a short course on Code Profiling and Optimization as part of a student-run initiative at IIT Gandhinagar.
